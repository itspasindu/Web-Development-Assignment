Welcome to Sri Lanka 🍵

🏝️ Top Attractions

🏞️ Sigiriya Rock Fortress (Lion Rock)

Location: Central Province
Highlights:
🎭 A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress built in the 5th century by King Kasyapa.
🎭 Visitors can climb nearly 1,200 steps to reach the top, where the ruins of the king’s palace and breathtaking panoramic views await.
🎭 Famous features include the Frescoes (ancient wall paintings), Mirror Wall, and the Lion’s Paws carved into the rock.
🎭 It is often called the 8th Wonder of the World due to its historical, architectural, and artistic significance.

🚂 Ella – The Scenic Hill Town

Location: Uva Province
Highlights:
🎭 Ella is a charming town surrounded by hills covered with tea plantations and cloud forests.
🎭 The train ride to Ella from Kandy or Nuwara Eliya is one of the most scenic in the world.
🎭 Top sights include Nine Arches Bridge, Ella Rock, and Little Adam’s Peak.
🎭 Ideal for hikers, nature lovers, and those seeking peaceful mountain retreats.

🏰 Galle Fort – Colonial Elegance by the Sea

Location: Southern Province
Highlights:
🎭 Galle Fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and later fortified by the Dutch.
🎭 It's a charming coastal town with narrow cobblestone streets, colonial architecture, boutique shops, cafes, and museums.
🎭 The Fort walls offer beautiful sunset views over the Indian Ocean.
🎭A great blend of history, culture, and modern lifestyle.

🐋 Whale Watching in Mirissa

Location: Southern Coast
Highlights:
🎭 Mirissa is one of the best places in the world to spot blue whales, the largest animals on Earth.
🎭 Whale watching tours operate mainly from November to April when sightings are most frequent.
🎭 Dolphins, sperm whales, and sea turtles can also be seen.
🎭 The beaches in Mirissa are also perfect for relaxing, surfing, and enjoying beachside seafood.

🥘 Taste the Flavors of Sri Lanka

Savor a Culinary Adventure Like No Other Sri Lankan cuisine is a vibrant blend of spices, tropical ingredients, and age-old traditions. Whether you’re enjoying a street-side hopper or a feast of rice and curry, every bite tells a story.

Kottu Roti

A spicy chopped flatbread stir-fry with vegetables, egg, or meat.

Sri Lankan Crab Curry

Rich, spicy, and world-famous.

Hoppers

Bowl-shaped pancakes made from rice flour, crispy on the edges and soft in the center.

Buffet-style Rice and Curry

A daily staple with various sides like dhal, fish, and vegetables.
